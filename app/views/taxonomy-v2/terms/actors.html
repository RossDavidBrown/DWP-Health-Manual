Actors

Actors are the people using or delivering the service.

Actors include citizens engaging with a service, people engaging with a service on behalf of another citizen, DWP employees and more.

Our services have multiple user groups. We need to design our services to meet the needs of all users - not just Citizen users, but the Agents and Professionals that interact with or help deliver the service to help achieve Citizen outcomes.

Examples of actors

Citizen

Acting on their own behalf to engage with DWP services

For example:

  • citizen

Acting on behalf of another citizen to engage with DWP services

For example:

  • informal acting body
  • private acting body
  • corporate acting body

Agent

DWP employee or person otherwise authorised to act on behalf of DWP to enable delivery of DWP services

For example:

  • telephone agent (DWP)
  • case manager (DWP)
  • translator (3rd party contractor)

Professional

Someone acting in a professional capacity in support of an outcome

For example:

  • medical professional
  • law professional
  • special educational needs professional
