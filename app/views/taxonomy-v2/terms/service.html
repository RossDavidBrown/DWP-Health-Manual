Service

A service helps users to do something. Public services exist because of underlying citizen needs, policy intent, user needs and the strategy of a particular organisation. Services help government achieve policy intent on behalf of its citizens, with whom it has a social contract. Services are best identified as verbs rather than nouns.

A service helps someone achieve a goal. A whole end-to-end service delivers outcomes for their users - it is everything the user and government needs to do to achieve the outcome.
