Life event

A Life Event is a specific event in a person’s life – this can be long-term or short term, positive or negative. These are trigger points for a user journey or the underlying problem that someone is trying to solve. They are the reason that people interact with or contact your organisation.

Examples within DWP Health include losing a job, entering retirement or ill health.

Life events are an approach to designing services to meet people’s needs. It will help you understand the context, circumstances and what people do, think and feel in different situations.
