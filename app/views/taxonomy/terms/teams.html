Teams

How we currently organise our resources, capabilities and capacity.

Practice / profession

Leads for each practice / profession responsible for learning and development, quality and standards for practice areas.

Strategy teams

Product, data, tech and delivery strategy teams collaborate to ensure that the deliver, data and tech strategies underpin the overarching product strategy.

Service design teams

Maintain a single view of their product / theme. Work with other service design teams, strategy teams and product teams to ensure value creation, alignment and inform prioritisation.

Product teams

A self-organising team of multi-skilled individuals, working on a problem and delivering a set of outcomes withing the context of the end o end service design.

Enabling teams

A group of individual teams (plaform, infrastructure and end to end testing) working in a problem and delivering a set of outcomes to support, facilitate and protect our product teams.

Product support teams

Support application users by monitoring system performance via incident, customer contact and monistoring tools to help minimise issues and disruption to business as usual activities.
