User journey

A User Journey can be described by the citizen’s experience throughout the end-to-end service from their point of view. The narrative includes what users are doing, thinking, feeling and what they are interacting with along the way.

Mapping user journeys help you see and evaluate the experience is having from their point of view. From here you can surface and unpack the impact the organisation has on this journey.
