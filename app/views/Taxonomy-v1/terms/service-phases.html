Service phases

Where we can break services up into key steps of the overall proces

These stages may be provided by a number of different providers, but to the user, a service is one continuous set of actions towards their goal regardless of who is providing it.

  1. Find out.
    2. Citizen finds out information and ways to get help. Understand options and choose what to apply for.

  2. Apply.
    3. Citizen fills out and submist application to make a claim and provides evidence.

  3. Decide.
    4. DWP processes and routes application, if necessary refers to assessment. DWP decides an outcome and informs citizen.

  4. Dispute.
    5. Citizen may dispute an outcome, leading to Mandatory Reconsideration and possibly Appeal.

  5. Pay.
    6. Citizen receives award.

  6. Review.
    7. Udpdates on any change of circumstances or award reviews, as well as support to move on from a particular benefit.
