Benefits and grants

Financial support awarded and discharged by DWP.

Benefits and tax credits are payments from the government to certain people on low incomes, or to meet specific needs.

They can help you if you:

  • are on a low income
  • are out of work
  • have children
  • are pregnant
  • are sick or disabled
  • have been bereaved
  • are a carer

Health related benefits and grants

Benefits available for those with a health condition, or who care for a dependent that has a health condition, include:

  • Personal Independence Payment (PIP)
  • Employment Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Attendance Allowance
  • Disability Living Allowance
  • Carer's Allowance
  • Access to Work (AtW)
